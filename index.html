About Our School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence & Values

Pride International School has been a cornerstone of quality education since 2001. Affiliated with CBSE, we offer a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to Class 12, blending academic rigour with holistic development. Our campus is a vibrant community where curiosity is celebrated and every student is empowered to reach their highest potential.

Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-first philosophy make us one of the most trusted institutions in the region. We go beyond textbooks to instil critical thinking, creativity, and character — preparing students not just for examinations but for life.

  • CBSE Affiliated with consistent 100% board results
  • State-of-the-art smart classrooms & digital labs
  • Experienced faculty with child-centred teaching approach
  • Extensive co-curricular & sports programs for all-round growth

Academics

Programs We Offer

From early childhood to senior secondary — we offer thoughtfully designed programs that inspire learning at every stage.

Pre-Primary (Nursery – UKG)

Play-based learning that builds social skills, language, and early numeracy through joyful exploration and creativity.

Primary School (Class 1 – 5)

A strong academic foundation with emphasis on reading, mathematics, science, and value-based education.

Middle School (Class 6 – 8)

Comprehensive CBSE curriculum with project-based learning, critical thinking, and introduction to specialised subjects.

Secondary (Class 9 – 10)

Board-focused preparation with expert guidance, regular assessments, and personalised mentoring for top results.

Senior Secondary – Science (11–12)

Physics, Chemistry, Biology & Mathematics with dedicated labs and JEE/NEET oriented coaching support.

Senior Secondary – Commerce (11–12)

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics & Mathematics preparing students for CA, MBA and commerce careers.

Our Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure

Every facility at Pride International School is designed with one purpose — to give our students the very best environment to learn, grow, and excel.

Modern Library

A vast collection of 15,000+ books, journals, e-resources, and a dedicated reading zone that nurtures a lifelong love for learning.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern instruments enabling hands-on experimental learning.

Sports & Athletics

Olympic-standard cricket ground, basketball court, badminton hall, swimming pool, and professional coaching staff.

Smart Classrooms

Every classroom is equipped with interactive smart boards, high-speed internet, and digital learning tools for an immersive experience.

Safe Transport

A fleet of 18 GPS-tracked, air-conditioned school buses covering all major routes with trained drivers and attendants.

Hygienic Cafeteria

A spacious, FSSAI-certified canteen serving nutritious, freshly prepared meals and snacks in a clean and welcoming environment.
